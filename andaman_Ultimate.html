Hotel is across the road from your Apr 29 jetty — no cab needed that morning.

🌊 Corbyn's Cove Beach First Swim FREE · Auto ₹80
10-min auto ride, 4 km south of Aberdeen. White sand, calm waves, safe zone. Walk in barefoot — no agenda. Float on your back and look at the sky. This is the ocean welcoming you. 45 min. 🏖️ Fun: Find 3 different types of shells. The one with the most unusual shape becomes your trip mascot.
5:30 PM
🚩 Flag Point — Panoramic Sunset Sunset 1 FREE
3-min walk from Corbyn's Cove. Hilltop view of the entire Port Blair harbour, Ross Island strait, and open sea. Sunset at 6:18 PM — arrive by 5:30 PM. The sun drops behind layered island silhouettes. 📸 Pose: "Charlie's Angels" group silhouette. Group jump shot at golden light. Everyone pointing in different directions ("island explorers" pose).
7:00 PM
🍛 Dinner — Aberdeen Bazaar Veg ✅ ₹120–200/person
Best veg tonight: Annapurna Restaurant — pure veg thali ₹120–150, always crowded = always fresh. Dal, sabzi, rice, chapati. After dinner: walk the bazaar (alive 7–9 PM). Peek into the antique lane — old coins, tribal replica artifacts, shell collections. Sri Durga Mandir just off the main road (old, local, quietly beautiful). Don't buy souvenirs — Neil market prices are 40% cheaper.
8:45 PM
🔦 Cellular Jail — Light & Sound Show Sunday = OPEN ₹100 English show
English show 8:45 PM · Hindi 7:30 PM. This Sunday slot is the ONLY chance for the L&S show — jail is closed Monday (Day 2) and you fly at 9 AM on May 6. Pre-book at andamantourism.gov.in today on hotel WiFi. The jail walls are lit; freedom fighters narrate their story. One of the most spine-tingling things India has. Sleep in silence after. Tomorrow starts at 7 AM — no rush.
10:30 PM
🌙 Sleep · Alarm 7:00 AM · Ask Hotel: Wandoor Cab 9:30 AM Tomorrow
Ask hotel front desk to arrange a cab to Wandoor Jetty for 9:30 AM tomorrow (₹400–500 total for the group). Confirm Jolly Buoy 10:30 AM ferry slot.

Jolly Buoy Island · Living Coral · Sand Games · Work Block

"No plastic. No rush. The best coral reef in India."
7:00 AM
☕ Sunrise + Chai at Phoenix Bay Waterfront Sunrise FREE
Sunrise 5:28 AM — hotel faces east over Phoenix Bay. Quick walk outside to watch the harbour wake up: fishing boats heading out, ferry crews preparing. Chai from the roadside stall (₹10–15). Morning music on the radio. Pack your Jolly Buoy tiffin box: snacks in cloth bag/tiffin (no plastic on island), steel water bottle, reef-safe sunscreen, original ID.
8:00 AM
🍽️ Breakfast Veg ✅ ₹80–150

9:30 AM
🚗 Cab to Wandoor Jetty (30 km) ₹400–500 total
45-min coastal drive. Arrive Wandoor by 10:15 AM. Buy forest permit at counter: ₹75/person. Board Jolly Buoy ferry at 10:30 AM.

11:15 AM
🐠 Coral Snorkeling + Glass-Bottom Boat + Beach Games Marine World
Glass-bottom boat (₹200) — visible coral from a dry seat. Snorkeling (₹1,000–2,500) — clownfish, parrotfish, giant clams in 1–2m water. Living coral visible standing. Do NOT touch coral — ₹10,000 fine, strictly enforced. After snorkeling: 🏰 Sand castle competition — teams of 2, 30 min, random passerby judges. Mermaid burial — sculpt a tail around whoever lies down, photograph overhead. Tiffin lunch under the trees at 12:30 PM. Cards on the beach after eating.
2:15 PM
🚤 Board Return Boat (all aboard by 2:15 PM) ⚠️ Island closes 2 PM
Back at Wandoor ~3 PM. Tender coconut from stall (₹40–50). Cab back to Hotel Oceanview — arrive ~4 PM.

Back at hotel, fresh up, open laptop. Hotel Oceanview Phoenix Bay has reliable WiFi. 2-hour focused work session in cool AC while the afternoon heat makes outdoor time unpleasant anyway. Personal snacks: dry fruits, energy bar at the desk.
6:30 PM
🛍️ Aberdeen Bazaar + Samudrika Museum (if open) Antiques
Antique curio lane off the main bazaar — old shell collections, replica tribal items, British-era artifacts. Sagarika Government Emporium (authentic crafts, fixed prices). Chatham Saw Mill (1836) is Asia's oldest working sawmill — on Chatham Island via bridge from PB, open 9 AM–11:30 AM weekdays. If you want to see it, it fits on Day 2 morning before Wandoor (9–9:15 AM quick stop).
8:00 PM
🍛 Dinner + Pack for Havelock Veg ✅
New Lighthouse Restaurant (good veg options, paneer, dal, ₹150–250) or Annapurna again. After dinner: pack all bags for Havelock. Everything ready by the door. Alarm set for 4:30 AM — Makruzz is tomorrow.
9:30 PM
🌙 Sleep — Alarm 4:30 AM ⚠️ Early start tomorrow
7 hours of sleep before the 6 AM ferry. Hotel is 60 seconds from the jetty — genuinely the most convenient possible setup for this departure.
💚 Port Blair Hacks
  • Cellular Jail day visit is not possible on this trip — Closed Mon (Day 2), you ferry at 6 AM Tue (Day 3), Closed Mon again (Day 9), 9 AM flight May 6 (Day 10). The L&S show tonight is your only window. Book it before sleeping on Day 1.
  • Hotel Oceanview location = superpower. Phoenix Bay Jetty is literally across the road. Apr 29: walk out at 5:20 AM, no cab, no stress.
  • Jolly Buoy permit: ask hotel to pre-arrange the night before — saves the Wandoor counter queue.
  • Reef-safe sunscreen only on Jolly Buoy — ₹10,000 fine for regular sunscreen. Or wear a rash guard instead (much better protection anyway).
  • Sugarcane juice from Aberdeen street vendors: ₹20–30, freshly pressed, incredible.

Kalapathar Sunrise · Scuba Diving · Sea Walk · Radhanagar

"All three layers — sky at dawn, surface at sunset, 12 metres deep."
5:00 AM
🌅 Kala Pathar Beach — Sunrise ⭐ 4.7 Sunrise 1 FREE
6 km east, 15-min scooty ride in the dark. Arrive before 5:25 AM sunrise. Black volcanic rocks, white sand — completely alone. The Bay of Bengal cracks open in orange. Bring thermos chai + personal snacks (biscuit, dry fruit). Stay until 6:30 AM. 📸 Rock throne: Sit on the highest black rock, arms spread, facing the sunrise. "Jump shot series" with orange sky behind. The most Instagram-worthy unposed sunrise you'll ever have.
7:00 AM
🍳 Light Breakfast · Dive Prep Veg ✅ · Eat VERY light
Banana, toast, eggs only — no heavy food before diving (nausea at depth). Head to Barefoot Scuba Beach 3 by 8 AM for paperwork. Pre-call them last night: +91 9531864680.
8:30 AM
🤿 SCUBA DIVING — Fun Dive 12 Metres Deep ₹3,200–4,000/person
20-min briefing + full gear + PADI guide + 40-45 min underwater at 9–12m. You'll see: coral gardens, moray eels, giant clams, lionfish, possibly nurse sharks (harmless). Complete silence. Weightlessness. The coral world from inside it. GoPro rental ₹500–800 — worth it. Book scuba + sea walk together = 15–20% combo discount. Mention any ear/sinus issues. ✅ Scuba Apr 30 → Flight May 6 = 6-day gap. Completely safe.
11:30 AM
🍛 Lunch — Eat Well Veg ✅ ₹200–350
Post-dive you need real food + aggressive hydration. No alcohol today. Full Moon Café (paneer, dal, veg thali) is excellent. Personal snacks as a top-up.
1:00 PM
💼 WORK BLOCK + Post-Dive Rest (2 hrs) Workation
Post-dive rest is mandatory AND it's 33°C outside. Work in the AC room 1–3 PM. Perfect natural alignment — your body needs the downtime anyway. Drink water continuously.
3:30 PM
🚶 Underwater Sea Walk — Helmet Dive Walk on the Sea Floor ₹2,500–3,500/person
Walk on the actual sea floor at 5–7m with a pressurized helmet — normal breathing, no swimming needed. 25–30 min. You walk between fish and coral. Different from scuba — you're strolling rather than swimming. Book with same operator as scuba for combo discount.
5:30 PM
🌅 Radhanagar — Second Sunset Sunset 3 FREE
The ocean looks different now that you've been inside it. The second Radhanagar visit tends to be more contemplative. 📸 Underwater theme tonight: "Newspaper deep" (someone reading chest-deep), underwater phone-pouch shots. Dinner at resort after. Cards in the evening. Sleep by 10 PM.

"Tides Near Me" app or ask Samsara staff. Natural Bridge only visible at low tide. If low tide window is 4–7 PM today: go now. Eyes closed 3 min to dark-adapt, then open — you'll see 3× more stars. Cards on the beach: Name as many constellations as you can. Loser buys chai tomorrow.

🌌 Stargazing Guide — Neil Island
  • Best spots: Bharatpur Beach (Day 7) or TSG Aura private beach (Day 8) — minimal light, max sky
  • Best window: 9:30 PM – midnight · Milky Way core highest overhead in late April–May
  • Moon phase: New moon ~May 1 = very dark sky by May 3–5 ✅
  • What to find: Milky Way core (dense stellar cloud), Scorpius constellation (bright in May southern sky), Jupiter/Saturn if visible, Orion setting west
  • Phone photography: Night Mode, 3–5 sec exposure, rest phone flat on backpack. Turn all phone screens away.
  • Close eyes for 3 min first (dark adapt) — you'll see exponentially more stars when you open them
